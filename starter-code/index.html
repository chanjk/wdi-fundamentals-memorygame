Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso, or Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Why you should play this game

This game is suitable for anyone, be it young children or old adults. A good memory is essential in many parts of our lives, and regular practice helps to keep it sharp, even in old age. Most people also enjoy the challenge games like this bring, with competitions being held in many countries on a regular basis. Say No! to forgetfulness, improve your memory now!
